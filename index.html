About Me

Hi! My name is Rylie and I am a student at Sierra College and study graphic design, illustration, and digital media!

Alongside being a student, I am also a tutor for the Applied Art and Design program offered at Sierra College and tutor many subjects including 3D modeling, Animation, Graphic Design, Digital Imaging, and more! I enjoy the opportunity to help others with similar interests and also be able to provide a support for others to help them succeed now and prepare them for future opportunites!

I love being creative and combining my love for art and illustration with design and function! Collaborating and communicating with others provides wonderful experiences and opportunites to create work to influence others for good! Moving forward, I plan on developing my web design skills to further my work as an artist so I can provide a positive impact to those who connect with my work.
